ABOUT ZTA

Who We Are

ZTA began as a personal passion project — capturing the cosmos from a small rooftop in the heart of the city, battling light pollution with nothing but determination and custom-built gear.

Today, it has grown into a movement: proving that anyone, anywhere, can reach the stars with creativity, precision engineering, and relentless curiosity.

Every image is 100% real — no simulations, no stock photos. Just photons that traveled billions of years to meet a hand-crafted telescope under a polluted sky.
