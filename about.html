ABOUT ZTA

From a Rooftop to the Edge of the Universe

Who We Are

ZTA began as a personal passion project — capturing the cosmos from a small rooftop in the heart of the city, battling light pollution with nothing but determination and custom-built gear.

Today, it has grown into a movement: proving that anyone, anywhere, can reach the stars with creativity, precision engineering, and relentless curiosity.

Every image is 100% real — no simulations, no stock photos. Just photons that traveled billions of years to meet a hand-crafted telescope under a polluted sky.

Our Journey

2019 – First Light

Built first imaging rig from scrap parts. Captured M31 Andromeda with a modified DSLR.

2021 – Custom Telescope

Designed and machined a 10-inch Newtonian with zero-backlash mount. First deep-sky masterpiece.

2023 – International Recognition

Featured by NASA APOD, ESA, and awarded in global astrophotography competitions.

2025 – ZTA Community

Launched platform to inspire the next generation of urban astrophotographers worldwide.
